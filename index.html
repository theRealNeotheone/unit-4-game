Crystals Collector!

Start with random number at the beginning of the game.

There are four crystals as you can see. Click on a crystal to add points to your total score.

Win the game by matching your total score to the random number. Lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
